About BYD Seal

We bring electric innovation to life through precision engineering and intelligent design. Recognized with the iF Design Award 2023, trusted by drivers who demand performance, efficiency, and refined comfort.

BYD Seal Exterior Design

Ocean Aesthetics Design

The front of the BYD Seal follows the X design concept, paired with a dynamic and flowing waistline that reflects its ocean-inspired aesthetic. Every curve is carefully shaped to enhance both aerodynamics and visual elegance, creating a seamless balance between performance and style.

BYD Seal Interior Design

Crafted for Every Moment Inside

Step inside the BYD Seal and experience a cabin shaped by innovation and comfort. A signature rotatable touchscreen adapts to your needs, while the panoramic sunroof fills the space with natural light. Integrated sport seats provide support and elegance, complemented by smart storage that keeps everything in place. Together, these elements create a refined and immersive driving experience.
